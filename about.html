Hi, my name is Juan, I am a data scientist based in Pittsburgh, PA.

Hey there! I'm currently enrolled in a data-focused Master's degree in Public Policy at Carnegie Mellon University, and I hold a Master's degree in Applied Economics and a Bachelor's degree in Economics from Andes University. My passion for detecting patterns and insights in data and finding solutions to complex problems has led me to explore the exciting world of data science and public policy.

Throughout my career, I've had the chance to work in both the public and private sectors, including healthcare, national defense, natural gas market regulation, and finance. I've even managed to find some cost-effective tools for the health system by tinkering with technologies like R, SQL, Python, and JavaScript.

I'm a big believer in using data to drive informed decision-making and solving complex problems, and I'm always excited to take on new challenges. My experience working with complex systems has given me the ability to translate business questions into meaningful insights. As I continue on this journey, I look forward to making meaningful contributions and having fun along the way!
