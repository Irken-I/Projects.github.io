Hi, my name is Juan, I am a data scientist based in Pittsburgh, PA.

Greetings! I am a current graduate student pursuing a data-focused Master's degree in Public Policy at Carnegie Mellon University. My academic background includes a Master's degree in Applied Economics and a Bachelor's degree in Economics from Andes University. My enthusiasm for identifying patterns, drawing insights from data, and solving complex problems has led me to explore the exciting field of data science and its applications in public policy.

Throughout my career, I have worked across multiple sectors, including healthcare, national defense, natural gas market regulation, and finance, in both public and private capacities. I have utilized tools such as R, SQL, Python, and JavaScript to devise cost-effective solutions for the healthcare industry.

My approach is centered around using data to drive informed decision-making and tackle complex challenges. I am always eager to embrace new challenges and believe my experience working with complex systems equips me to provide valuable insights to business questions. I am excited to continue on this journey, make meaningful contributions and have fun along the way!
