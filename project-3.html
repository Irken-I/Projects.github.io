Covid Col

An interactive tool to model pandemic control policy outcomes bases in a SIER model

Consult the webpage Here

Through my adaptation of a web tool, which was based on the SIER model previously utilized by the Ministry of Finance of Colombia in 2020, I was able to model the potential epidemiological outcomes of different pandemic control policies. This tool provided valuable insights for policymakers, allowing them to make more informed decisions regarding effective policies to control the spread of COVID-19.

The SIER model is a mathematical model used to simulate the spread of an infectious disease within a population. The acronym SIER stands for Susceptible, Infected, Exposed, and Recovered. In this model, individuals within a population are categorized into one of these four groups based on their current state in relation to the disease.
